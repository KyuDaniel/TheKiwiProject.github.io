    The Kiwi Project


    A Student-led Initiative

    The Kiwi Project simply began as a school project founded in November 2023. The organization decided to advocate safe spaces as a way to support students’ or individuals’ freedom of expression and to educate acceptance of others despite differences in age, gender, color, and other factors. Furthermore, they have chosen to highlight one of the several factors that threaten the confidentiality of Safe Space, which is harassment based on gender.


    SAFE SPACES

    Safe Spaces is a haven in which individuals of different genders, ages, races, socioeconomic classes, and experiences are accepted, appreciated, treated equally with open minds, and may express themselves freely without fear of prejudice or condemnation. It is based at Miriam College Community. It aims to foster and educate the residents of the school regardless of age to create a safer environment wherein everyone feels secure and valued. It helps numerous individuals freely express themselves and showcase their true identity without the fear of being judged. With this, many can gain confidence and speak up for themselves.


    GENDER BASED VIOLENCE

    Gender-based violence is violence committed against a person because of his or her sex or gender; this involves the use of force, deceit, violence, cultural norms, or financial gain to compel someone else to act against their will. It comes in different forms, it can be sexual, physical, verbal, psychological, or socioeconomic.
